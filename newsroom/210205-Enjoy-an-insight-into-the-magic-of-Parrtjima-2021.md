The magic of Parrtjima – A Festival in Light 2021 will be shared with audiences far and wide via an online preview on Monday, 8 February. It is the first time fans have been given an insight into what’s in store through an online event.

Set to begin at 2pm AEDT, the preview will feature some of the awe-inspiring 2021 installations, as well as introduce some of the world-class artists involved and the experts responsible for transforming the two-dimensional artworks into sensational three-dimensional light sculptures.

Parrtjima is delivered by NT Major Events Company and produced by Creative Directors AGB Events. This year’s festival will take place at Alice Springs Desert Park and Todd Mall from 9-18 April 2021.
