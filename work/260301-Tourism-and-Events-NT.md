Access has been the PR agency of record for Tourism and Events NT since 2017, managing highly successful, always-on and major event PR across Australia and New Zealand.

Our work includes communications strategy and implementation, proactive and reactive media relations and publicity, famil planning and hosting, content creation, and ambassador projects.

Alongside destination publicity, we manage PR for the Territory’s most iconic major events including Parrtjima, BASSINTHEGRASS music festival, MXGP Australia, Million Dollar Fish and betr Darwin Triple Crown.

Much of our work involves working closely with Aboriginal cultures, communities, creatives, and talent. We bring not just respect, but a deep cultural awareness, understanding, and appreciation to everything we do.

As a trusted extension of Tourism and Event NT’s in-house team, we consistently exceed KPIs every year, driving significant awareness and consideration for the Northern Territory as a desirable holiday and business events destination.
