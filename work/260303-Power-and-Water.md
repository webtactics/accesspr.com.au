We have completed a raft of strategic communications across a number of divisions at Power and Water, as well as developing its current Aboriginal Engagement Strategy and Remote Community Unplanned Outages Communications manual.

We have also delivered a series of media and presentation training sessions over the past few years as well as working on high-level corporate strategic projects.

Our work involves working closely with senior management across sometimes sensitive issues and themes, as well as developing mainstream brand creative campaigns.
