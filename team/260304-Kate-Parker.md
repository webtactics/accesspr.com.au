Kate is a senior PR and communications professional with over 14 years’ experience working across some of Australia’s most recognisable tourism and lifestyle brands.

She has led national and global consumer PR campaigns for Intrepid Travel, Bannisters Hotels, Vail Resorts, Lexus, Vision Australia, Starlight Children's Foundation and more.

Kate maintains close working relationship with many of Australia’s key travel, lifestyle and culture media. Fuelled by a genuine love of storytelling, Kate believes that when it comes to communications, authentic connection is more important than ever.
